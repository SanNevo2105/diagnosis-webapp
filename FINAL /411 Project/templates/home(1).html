WELCOME TO QUICK+, your medical decision assistant!

QUICK+ allows you to easily search up information about many diseases, as well as input your symptoms to find out what diseases you may have through a ML model that links symptoms to most likely disease!

Finally, you can also see what diseases and symptoms are prevalent in your area and better take precautions against them!

To begin using the application, select one of the following three options:

  1. Find out about a disease!
  2. Input your symptoms to see what you have!
  3. Learn about prevalent symptoms in your area!
